Gift distribution

Yesterday, Sunday 14th April, our volunteers distributed the recycled toys at Saint-Antoine hospital in Paris.

After collecting waste from various companies, our employees worked hard to recycle it into a toy for sick children living at Saint-Antoine Hospital. The distribution was a succes and the children have now new toys to distract them from they're treatment. We expect to do another distribution next month to another hospital.

A special thanks to our volonteer, Nadia and Theo who took care of the transportation of the toys and they're distribution.




Opening of a new recycling facility

We are proud to announce the opening of a new facility in england within the year thank to the signing of new contract with the London branch of lego and AC Plastics.

This new facility will be construct in the London suburbs and the recycled product distributed throughout England. We are looking for new volonteer, do not hesitate to contact us if you are interested.
